Welcome to Rosewood Resorts

Rosewood Hotels & Resorts is a renowned luxury hotel and resort management company with a global presence. Our Restaurant

Rosewood Hotels & Resorts offer world-class dining experiences, often featuring gourmet cuisine with a focus on locally sourced ingredients. Their restaurants are known for their exceptional culinary offerings and elegant ambiance.

Italian Kitchen

Italian Kitchen at Rosewood Hotels & Resorts is a culinary haven for lovers of Italian cuisine. With an extensive menu of traditional and modern Italian dishes, it offers a delectable journey through the flavors of Italy. Guests can savor handmade pasta, wood-fired pizza, and a fine selection of Italian wines in a warm and inviting atmosphere.

Mexican Kitchen

The Mexican Kitchen at Rosewood Hotels & Resorts invites guests to savor the vibrant and rich flavors of Mexico. It serves authentic Mexican dishes prepared with fresh ingredients and bold spices, providing an immersive culinary journey. Guests can enjoy traditional tacos, enchiladas, and a variety of margaritas in a lively and culturally inspired setting.

Indian Kitchen

The Indian Kitchen is a culinary treasure trove of diverse flavors and spices. Known for its aromatic curries, tandoori dishes, and a rich tapestry of vegetarian and non-vegetarian offerings, it offers an exploration of the subcontinent's cuisine. From the creamy indulgence of butter chicken to the fiery heat of vindaloo, Indian cuisine caters to a wide range of palates.

Chinese Kitchen

The Chinese Kitchen is a flavorful world of diverse regional cuisines. It encompasses everything from the fiery dishes of Sichuan to the delicate dim sum of Cantonese cuisine, showcasing the art of stir-frying and steaming with fresh ingredients and bold flavors. Chinese cuisine is celebrated for its rich history and culinary mastery.
